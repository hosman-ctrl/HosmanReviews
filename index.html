How to Use Hosman Movies

Navigation

Use the navigation bar at the bottom to switch between sections: Trending movies, Movies This Year, TV content, and Help.

Calendar View

In the "Movies This Year" section, view movies organized by month. Click on any day with movies to see what's releasing.

Movie Details

Tap any movie poster to see detailed information, including description, rating, and release date.

TV Section

Browse all content types including movies, series, and short films. Use the genre filters and search bar to find specific content.

Notifications

For upcoming movies, you can add them to your calendar to get reminders when they're released.

Infinite Scroll

Scroll down in any section to automatically load more content. The app will continue loading as you reach the bottom.

Themes

The app automatically adapts to your Telegram theme. You can also manually switch between light and dark modes in your Telegram settings.

Sharing

Share your favorite movies with friends directly from the movie details view.
