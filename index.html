About MovieMate

AI Recommendations

Our AI analyzes your preferences and searches through thousands of movies to find the perfect matches for you. Just describe what you're looking for, and we'll do the rest!

Movie Information Source

All movie data is provided by The Movie Database (TMDb). This includes details like ratings, release dates, cast information, and movie summaries.

AI Technology

We use Google's Gemini AI to generate detailed movie summaries and provide personalized recommendations based on your preferences.

Note: Our AI is still under development and may require fine-tuning. Some responses might not be perfect yet.

Year Filtering

Use the year filter buttons to explore movies from different time periods. We automatically show the current year's movies, but you can explore previous and upcoming years too.

Randomizer

Can't decide what to watch? Use the Randomize button at the bottom to get a surprise selection of movies from different genres and years!
